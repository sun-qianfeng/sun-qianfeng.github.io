Discover the unparalleled dining experience at the Abbott Hotel. Our esteemed culinary venues offer guests and locals alike the opportunity to indulge in delectable dishes and beverages at the Restaurant, Lounge, Marketplace, and Café . In-room dining is also available for your convenience.


Restaurant Savor an unforgettable gastronomic journey from dawn to dusk, featuring fresh ingredients sourced from the Gourmet Marketplace. Guests can relish exquisite meals and eagerly return for any occasion.



The ambiance is a captivating fusion of vintage glamour and contemporary flair, reminiscent of the golden age of jazz and the progressive spirit of the 21st century. The lounge exudes an alluring energy, boasting vibrant shades of blue and gold.



The Gourmet Marketplace presents a unique concept for hotel dining, offering a fine selection of fresh seasonal produce, gourmet meals, and artisanal products. In addition, the marketplace features a butcher and seafood shop, a bakery, a patisserie, a crepe station, and much more.



This charming café comes to life with the delightful aromas of freshly baked goods and ground coffee beans. Unwind by the cozy fireplace or observe the bustling city through the café's expansive windows. Explore the distinct flavours of beverages crafted by the talented baristas at the Café , creating a memorable experience for all.
